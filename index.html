Smart Bell For Smart Generation

Smart Bell for Smart Generation is an innovative automatic bell system developed on the Arduino platform to provide a smart and flexible school bell solution. This device is designed to overcome the limitations of conventional bells by offering two main features: audio customization and programmable scheduling.

In this project, I served as the Project Manager with full responsibility for overseeing the project lifecycle from planning to final product. My primary contributions included designing the project timeline, defining clear feature scope, and efficiently allocating tasks to hardware and software teams.

Psychology & Humanities Journal

Psychoxi is a digital journal platform exploring the intersection of Psychology and the Humanities. Its mission is to deliver insightful, intelligent, accessible, and inclusive content that bridges modern, accessible, and user-centric digital experiences.

In this project, I held a dual role as a UI/UX Designer and Front-End Developer. I was fully responsible for the entire visual design and user experience process, from researching reader needs to creating wireframes and implementing responsive designs across all devices.

Crafting Digital Presence

This project is a minimalist and artistic portfolio website for a music group, designed as a digital stage to showcase their identity and musical work. Its goal is to provide an intimate and straightforward experience, where a clean aesthetic highlights the band's personality and the audio quality of their songs without distraction.

In this project, I acted as the sole UI/UX Designer and Developer. I was responsible for the entire process, from translating the band's identity into a visual concept to coding the final implementation with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, prioritizing a seamless music player and intuitive navigation.
